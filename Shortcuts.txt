Shortcuts:

	Annotation Window:
		UP: Move to previous task.
		DOWN: Move to next task.
		Ctrl+DOWN: Accept original translation and move to next task.
		Alt+UP: Move to previous task while in editing mode.
		Alt+DOWN: Move to next task while in editing mode.
		HOME: Move to first task.
		END: Move to next unedited task.
		Ctrl+F: Open search box.
		
	Assessment Window:
		ENTER: Accept the assessment and close the window.
	
	Search Window:
		ESC: Close search window.
		LEFT: Move to previous task with a match.
		RIGHT: Move to next task with a match.
		Ctrl+LEFT: Move to first task with a match.
		Ctrl+RIGHT: Move to last task with a match.
